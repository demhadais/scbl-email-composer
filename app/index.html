SCBL Email Composer

The Jackson Laboratory — Single Cell Biology Lab
How to use:
  1. Select a template tab above
  2. Fill in the form fields below
  3. Review the live preview on the right
  4. Click Copy Email Body to Clipboard
  5. In Outlook, compose a new email and paste (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V)
  6. Copy the subject line, add recipients, and send
